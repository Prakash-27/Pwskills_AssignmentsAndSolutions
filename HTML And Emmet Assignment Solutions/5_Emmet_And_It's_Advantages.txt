5. What is emmet? List some of the advantages emmet offers?

Emmet is a plugin for many popular text editors which greatly improves HTML & CSS workflow
Emmet is a free add-on for your text editor. It allows you to type shortcuts that are then expanded into full pieces of code. 

Emmet Advantages:
i)Developers type less.
ii)They save both on keystrokes and time. iii)Leading to more robust and valid files.
iv)Increased productivity and Faster coding.
